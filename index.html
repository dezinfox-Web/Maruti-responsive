Frequently Asked Questions

High-quality, reliable solutions tailored to your industrial needs. Find answers to our most common queries below.

What is maize starch?

Maize starch is a fine, white powder derived from corn, widely used in food processing, textiles, paper, and industrial applications due to its binding and thickening properties.

What industries use your products?

Our products serve food and beverage, textiles, paper, and pharmaceuticals industries with specialized starch solutions for each.

What is your minimum order quantity?

Our standard minimum order quantity is 1 ton (1000 kg). For trial or specialized orders, we can discuss tailored quantities.

Do you provide bulk supply?

Yes, we specialize in high-volume bulk supply with capacity to deliver 100+ metric tons per month with consistent quality and reliable logistics.

Are your products certified?

Yes, we are FSSAI certified, MSME registered, HALAL certified, and adhere to strict ISO 9001:2015 quality standards.
